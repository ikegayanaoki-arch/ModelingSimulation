This lesson covers programs that solve the 2D steady-state heat conduction equation using the finite difference method.

1. Basic Exercise (2d.Ans.py)

This program solves a 2D steady-state heat conduction problem where the side walls are held at a high temperature and the top/bottom walls at a low temperature.
